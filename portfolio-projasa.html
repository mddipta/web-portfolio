Projasa

Projasa is a website of a service bureau company that offers various services.

I am creating a website for two types of users: clients and admins. The front page is used by clients. Clients can obtain information about services, KBLI (Indonesian Standard Industrial Classification), company names, and more.

On the admin page, admins can create orders, generate invoices, create quotations, and monitor order graphs over specific periods. Admins can also edit the content on the front page.
